Hi, I’m Jessica.

I’ve spent my career navigating the complexities of high-scale technology, while supporting a culture of continuous improvement and learning. Whether I’m overseeing the architecture for a large organisation or stabilising a legacy monolith, my goal is always the same: to leave things in a better state than I found them, both the code and the culture.

Why Work With Me?

I’ve worked across the full spectrum of company sizes and maturities, primarily in highly regulated environments. My expertise lies at the intersection of technical strategy and cultural change.

Relevant Qualifications

  • TOGAF Enterprise Architecture - Foundation (10th Edition)
  • TOGAF Enterprise Architecture - Practitioner (10th Edition)
  • Microsoft Most Valuable Professional - Developer Technologies (2019 - 2025)

My Approach: Consultative, Collaborative, and Sustainable

I don’t believe in "architecture in an ivory tower." I believe in delivery. As a 5-time Microsoft MVP and a TOGAF-certified practitioner, I have the technical depth to speak the language of engineering. But as a former Head of Architecture and Board Member of the .NET Foundation, I understand that the most beautiful architecture in the world is useless if the team isn't bought in or if the business can't move the needle. When you hire me, you get:

  • Collaborative Insight::I provide the expertise to identify solutions, but I believe the best results come from working alongside your domain experts.
  • Empowered Implementation: :It is my responsibility to ensure your team understands the justification behind every proposal. As part of my roadmaps, I ensure the team has the necessary training and experience for the solution to be truly appropriate.
  • Transparency & Feedback:: I maintain clear communication lines and radical transparency. I garner feedback from stakeholders at all levels to ensure my proposals are practical, not just theoretical.
  • Value-Driven Change:: I want to ensure that every change provides value to the business without being detrimental to the experience of the teams supporting it.

Beyond the Screen

I’m a proud co-founder of the DDD East Midlands Conference and an active voice in the global tech community. When I’m not diving into architectural patterns, I’m likely advocating for better accessibility and inclusivity in tech—or recharging my "introverted soul" so I can show up as my best self for my clients and teams.

Let’s Build Something Sustainable

If you’re looking for someone who is as passionate about your business goals as they are about clean code and healthy teams, I’d love to talk.
