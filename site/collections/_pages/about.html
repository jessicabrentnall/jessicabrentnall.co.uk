Hi, I’m Jessica.

My work focuses on turning complex platforms into scalable, high-performing organisations. I’ve spent my career navigating the complexities of high-scale technology, while supporting a culture of continuous improvement and learning. Whether I’m overseeing the architecture for a large organisation or stabilising a legacy monolith, my goal is always the same: to leave things in a better state than I found them, both the code and the culture.

Why Work With Me?

I’ve worked across the full spectrum of company sizes and maturities, primarily in highly regulated environments. My expertise lies at the intersection of technical strategy and cultural change.

I understand that technical debt isn’t just a code problem, it’s a people problem. It’s about the teams who are maintaining the systems, the stakeholders who rely on them, and the business outcomes they enable. My approach is holistic: I focus on sustainable solutions that empower your teams and align with your business goals, rather than quick fixes that may lead to more debt down the line.

When we work together, you get a technical leader who bridges the gap between high-level strategy and deep-dive execution:

  • Collaborative & Technical Insight: I provide a range of technical expertise; with past experience including leading large-scale architectural initiatives. I work closely with your teams to understand their challenges and co-create solutions that are both technically sound and practically implementable.
  • Empowered Implementation: I don't dictate solutions with a one-size-fits-all approach; I focus on maintainability, stability, and security. I ensure your engineers understand the why behind every solution, empowering them to take ownership and drive continuous improvement long after our engagement ends.
  • Radical Transparency: Drawing on my experience as a senior technical leader, I maintain clear communication with stakeholders at every level. My proposals are battle-tested and practical, focused on solving real-world technical debt rather than chasing theoretical perfection.
  • Value-Driven, Sustainable Change: I focus on technical changes that move the needle for the business, whether that’s improving system resilience or passing rigorous audits, without compromising the developer experience or the mental well-being of the teams supporting the system.

Beyond the Screen

Throughout my career I've been actively involved in the tech community. I’ve spoken at conferences and meetups around the world, sharing insights on topics like stabilising "pre-loved" systems, fostering a culture of monitoring, and navigating the challenges of technical debt. I'm eager to return to the speaking circuit in the future. If you are interested in having me present about the work done during our engagement, we can discuss that.

I've also been the co-founder and co-organiser of events including meetups (Women In Tech Nottingham and .NET Notts). I'm most proud of the work founding and co-organising DDD East Midlands Conference with my husband Moreton. The conference was a not-for-profit event that ran for 5 years and was focused on supporting the local tech community and providing a platform for under-represented voices in tech.
